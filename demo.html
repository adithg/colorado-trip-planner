Trip Itinerary

Day 1: Plano, TX ‚Üí San Juan Mountains, CO

775 miles 12 hours
8:00 AM
Depart Plano, TX

Start your Colorado adventure! Make sure vehicle is ready for mountain driving.

12:00 PM - 2:00 PM
Palo Duro Canyon State Park

Second largest canyon in the US. Google Maps: 350 miles, 5h 30m from Plano. Don't miss the visitor center!

8:30-9:00 PM
Arrive in Telluride

Check into accommodation, late dinner. Google Maps: 425 miles, 6h 30m from Palo Duro!

Day 2: San Juan Mountains - Telluride Area

70 miles local 2 hours driving
8:00 AM
Bridal Veil Falls

Colorado's tallest free-falling waterfall (365 feet). 4WD recommended or moderate hike (3.5 miles roundtrip).

12:00 PM - 4:00 PM
Lower Blue Lakes Hike

3.5 miles roundtrip moderate hike near Ridgway. Stunning alpine lakes with dramatic mountain backdrop. Perfect for a lakeside picnic!

Evening
Return to Telluride

Explore Telluride town, gondola ride for sunset views (if open), dinner at local restaurant.

Day 3: San Juan Mountains ‚Üí Estes Park

375 miles 7.5 hours
8:00 AM - 12:00 PM
Million Dollar Highway (US-550)

One of America's most scenic drives through the San Juan Mountains. Spectacular mountain views and dramatic curves!

12:00 PM - 1:00 PM
Ouray - "Switzerland of America"

Lunch stop and optional hot springs visit. Beautiful Victorian-era town surrounded by towering peaks.

6:30 PM
Arrive in Estes Park

Check into accommodation, dinner in Estes Park. Google Maps: 325 miles, 6h from Ouray.

Day 4: Rocky Mountain National Park

60 miles local 2 hours driving
8:00 AM - 12:00 PM
Emerald Lake Trail

3.2 miles roundtrip moderate hike from Bear Lake. Passes Nymph Lake, Dream Lake, and Emerald Lake. Stunning alpine scenery!

1:00 PM - 5:00 PM
Trail Ridge Road & Alpine Visitor Center

Highest continuous paved road in US (12,183 ft peak). Alpine Visitor Center at 11,796 ft. Weather dependent - may be closed in winter!

Day 5: Estes Park ‚Üí Colorado Springs

180 miles 3 hours
11:30 AM - 3:00 PM
Garden of the Gods

FREE admission! Incredible red rock formations. Visit the visitor center, walk Balanced Rock and Cathedral Spires trails.

3:30 PM - 7:00 PM
Pikes Peak

14,115 ft summit! Drive the Pikes Peak Highway ($15) or take the Cog Railway. Don't miss the famous summit donuts!

Day 6: Colorado Springs ‚Üí Plano, TX

580 miles 8 hours
12:00 PM - 2:30 PM
Capulin Volcano National Monument

$7 entrance fee. Drive the 2-mile spiral road to the rim, walk the Crater Rim Trail. Unique volcanic landscape!

7:30 PM
Arrive home in Plano, TX
