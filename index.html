Evening
Return to Telluride

Gondola ride for sunset views, dinner

280 miles 4.5 hours

12:00 PM - 1:00 PM
Ouray - "Switzerland of America"

Lunch and optional hot springs visit

6:00 PM
Arrive in Estes Park

Check in, dinner, early rest

Day 4: Rocky Mountain National Park

60 miles local 2 hours driving
8:00 AM - 12:00 PM
Emerald Lake Trail

3.2 miles roundtrip. Passes Nymph, Dream, and Emerald Lakes.

1:00 PM - 5:00 PM
Trail Ridge Road & Alpine Visitor Center

Highest paved road in US (12,183 ft). Multiple scenic stops.

Day 5: Estes Park → Colorado Springs

180 miles 3 hours
11:30 AM - 3:00 PM

FREE admission. Red rock formations, visitor center, multiple trails.

3:30 PM - 7:00 PM
Pikes Peak

14,115 ft summit. Drive or Cog Railway. Famous summit donuts!

580 miles 8 hours
12:00 PM - 2:30 PM

$7 entrance fee. 2-mile spiral drive to rim, crater rim trail.

7:30 PM
