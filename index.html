6-Day Colorado Trip from Plano, Texas

Total Distance: 1,540 miles Total Driving: ~26 hours

Trip Itinerary

Day 1: Plano, TX → Colorado Springs, CO

580 miles 8 hours
6:00 AM
Depart Plano, TX
12:00 PM - 2:00 PM
Palo Duro Canyon State Park

Second largest canyon in the US. Google Maps: 350 miles, 5h 30m from Plano.

6:30-7:00 PM
Arrive in Colorado Springs

Check into accommodation, dinner. Google Maps: 280 miles, 4h 30m from Palo Duro.

Day 2: Pikes Peak → Garden of the Gods → Breckenridge

150 miles 3 hours driving
7:00 AM - 12:00 PM
Pikes Peak

14,115 ft summit. Drive or Cog Railway. Famous summit donuts!

12:30 PM - 4:00 PM
Garden of the Gods

FREE admission. Red rock formations, visitor center, multiple trails.

6:30 PM
Arrive in Breckenridge

Check in, dinner, early rest for Mount Quandary hike

Day 3: Mount Quandary Hike (14,265 ft)

26 miles total 1 hour driving
6:00 AM
Early Preparation

Breakfast, pack water, snacks, layers, rain gear

7:30 AM - 4:00 PM
Mount Quandary Fourteener Hike

6.75 miles roundtrip, 3,365 ft elevation gain. One of Colorado's easier 14ers!

Evening
Celebration Dinner

Rest and recovery after conquering your first fourteener!

Day 4: Breckenridge → Kayaking → Estes Park

195 miles 4 hours driving
8:00 AM
Depart Breckenridge

Drive to Arkansas River for kayaking adventure

10:00 AM - 3:00 PM
Whitewater Kayaking/Rafting

Class II-III rapids, guided trip with lunch included

6:00 PM
Arrive in Estes Park

Check in, dinner, early rest for Rocky Mountain National Park

Day 5: Rocky Mountain National Park

30 miles local 1 hour driving
8:00 AM - 12:00 PM
Emerald Lake Trail

3.2 miles roundtrip. Passes Nymph, Dream, and Emerald Lakes.

1:00 PM - 5:00 PM
Trail Ridge Road & Alpine Visitor Center

Highest paved road in US (12,183 ft). Multiple scenic stops.

Day 6: Estes Park → Plano, TX

615 miles 9.5 hours
8:00 AM
Depart Estes Park

Drive to Capulin Volcano National Monument

12:00 PM - 2:30 PM
Capulin Volcano National Monument

$7 entrance fee. 2-mile spiral drive to rim, crater rim trail.

8:00 PM
Arrive home in Plano, TX

End of amazing 6-day Colorado adventure!
